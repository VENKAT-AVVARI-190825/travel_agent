# Corporate Travel Policy

## Purpose
This policy governs how employees book and expense business travel. Agents that
plan trips MUST ground their recommendations in these rules. When a generated
plan conflicts with policy, the conflict must be surfaced to the traveller and
the approver.

## Cabin Class
- Flights under 6 hours: Economy class only.
- Flights of 6 hours or more: Premium Economy is permitted; Business class
  requires VP-level pre-approval.
- Upgrades purchased with personal funds or loyalty points are allowed and are
  not reimbursable.

## Booking Lead Time
- Domestic flights should be booked at least 14 days in advance.
- International flights should be booked at least 21 days in advance.
- Bookings made inside the lead-time window require a written business
  justification and manager approval.

## Preferred Suppliers
- Flights and hotels should be booked through preferred suppliers where
  available, as negotiated corporate rates apply.
- Non-preferred bookings are permitted only when they are at least 10% cheaper
  than the preferred option for a comparable itinerary.

## Payment
- All business travel must be paid on the corporate card. Personal cards should
  only be used when the corporate card is declined, and must be reconciled
  within 30 days.
- Cash advances are not permitted for travel.
