# Booking and Traveller Safety Policy

## Approval Workflow
- Every trip must have an approved plan before any booking is confirmed.
- Plans over 2,000 USD total require a second approver.
- The approval record must capture who approved, when, and the total estimated
  cost at time of approval.

## Traveller Safety
- Do not book travel to destinations under a company travel ban or a Level 4
  government travel advisory.
- For high-risk destinations, travellers must be registered with the security
  team before departure.
- Overnight layovers longer than 6 hours should include a hotel rather than an
  airport wait.

## Data and Privacy
- Traveller personal data (passport, date of birth, loyalty numbers) must not
  be stored in plan text or logs.
- Only the minimum data needed to complete a booking may be passed to
  suppliers.

## Sustainability
- Prefer direct flights and rail for journeys under 4 hours where a rail option
  exists.
- Consolidate trips where possible to reduce the number of separate journeys.
