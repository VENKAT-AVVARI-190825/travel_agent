# Expense and Reimbursement Policy

## Daily Allowances (per traveller, per day)
- Meals: up to 60 USD domestic, up to 90 USD international.
- Local transport (taxi, ride-share, transit): up to 40 USD per day.
- Incidentals: up to 15 USD per day.

## Hotels
- Standard room rate cap: 200 USD per night domestic, 300 USD per night
  international. Rates above the cap require manager approval and a note.
- Room service and minibar are not reimbursable.
- Hotel Wi-Fi and one checked bag are reimbursable.

## Receipts and Reconciliation
- Itemised receipts are required for any single expense of 25 USD or more.
- Expenses must be submitted within 30 days of trip completion.
- Corporate-card transactions must be matched to receipts before approval.

## Non-Reimbursable
- Personal entertainment, spa, and leisure activities not part of the business
  purpose.
- Fines, penalties, and traffic tickets.
- Travel insurance purchased separately (covered centrally by the company).
